Chronotope: Living Heritage in 4D

Using satellite time-series, D4RT 4D reconstruction, and Gaussian splatting to empower community-led conservation.

Core Insight

Chronotope produces a continuous, community-owned record of place by merging orbital views with street-level dynamics.

D4RT

The engine for feedforward 4D reconstruction, capturing living patterns at 200+ FPS.

Nimbo

Monthly satellite updates providing landscape-scale context for heritage sites.

Phases of Practice

Residents use smartphones to record daily life. D4RT handles camera motion and lighting changes automatically.

Captures are stitched into a 4D model where NeRF fills temporal gaps and 3DGS allows real-time viewing.
