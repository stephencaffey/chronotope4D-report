Phases of Heritage Practice

A workflow centered on data sovereignty and community agency.

1. Capture & Record

Communities use mobile devices and the D4RT engine to record dynamic scenes, preserving motion and geometry without heavy specialized equipment.

2. Accumulate & Reconstruct

Data is processed using NeRF to fill visual gaps and 3DGS for real-time rendering, creating a "living" digital archive.

3. Narrate & Advocate

These 4D models act as tools for education and evidence for conservation, owned and managed by the communities they represent.
